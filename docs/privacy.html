Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Scott Ruybalid ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Hanger mobile application ("App"). We aim to collect only what we need to run the App and to be transparent about everything we do.

1. What We Collect

Category Examples Why
Account info Email, handle, display name, hashed password Create and authenticate your account.
Profile content Kit photos, kit names, build notes, scores Power your hangar, the community feed, and AI scoring.
Subscription state Whether you have an active subscription, trial status, renewal date Gate paid features and apply your benefits.
Device / diagnostic Crash logs, app version, OS, anonymous usage events Diagnose bugs and improve the App. Never tied to your real-world identity.

We do not collect your contacts, precise location, advertising identifiers, or browsing history. We do not show third-party ads.

2. Who We Share With

We share data with the following service providers, and only as needed to run the App:

  • Supabase, Inc. — hosts our database, authentication, and photo storage.
  • Anthropic, PBC — receives photos and kit metadata when you submit a build for AI scoring. Anthropic does not train its models on your inputs by default under our API terms.
  • Apple and Google — process subscription payments. We never see your payment card details.
  • RevenueCat, Inc. — once subscriptions are live, will validate your receipts on our behalf.

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public.

3. How Long We Keep It

We keep your account data while your account is active and for up to 30 days after you delete it, to allow for backup recovery. Anonymous diagnostic data may be retained longer in aggregated form.

4. Your Rights

You can, at any time:

  • Access your data — visible in your profile and hangar within the App;
  • Update your account information — from the in-app profile settings;
  • Delete your account and associated data — from Settings → Account → Delete Account;
  • Export a copy of your data — email hangerapp.support@gmail.com and we will send it within 30 days.

If you are located in the EEA, UK, California, or other regions with specific privacy laws (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA), you may have additional rights, including the right to object to or restrict certain processing. Contact us to exercise them.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest, and apply database-level access controls so users can only see their own data and the public community feed. No system is perfectly secure; if a breach affects your data, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

7. International Transfers

The App is operated from the United States. Our service providers may process your data in the U.S. or other countries. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the App or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when changes were most recently made.

9. Contact

Privacy questions, data requests, or concerns? Email hangerapp.support@gmail.com.
